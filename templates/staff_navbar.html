Staff Help

Bookings

When a customer creates a booking, the booking will be shown on the Bookings page until it has been approved or denied by a staff member

Approve This will approve the booking and send a confirmation email to the user.

Deny This will deny the booking and send an email to the user saying their booking has been denied

Details This will load additional information about that specific booking

Check In

If a booking has been approved by a member of staff, the booking will then be shown on the Check In page if that booking is for today

Check In This will check the guests in and send a confirmation email to the user

No Show This will mark the guests as not turning up to the restaurant and will send an email informing them that they did not attend

If a guest does not show up and a staff member forgets to click No Show, then an automatic no show email will be sent to the user if the booking is in the past

Payments

If a booking has been approved and checked in by a member of staff, the booking will then be displayed on the Payments page. This is the last step of the life cycle of a booking.

Payment This will load a new page where a staff member can submit the payment information for that booking

Reviews

If a customer leaves a review, the review will be rendered on the Reviews page for a staff member to either approve or deny the review.

Approve This will approve the review and it will then be eligible to be rendered on the home page of the website

Deny This will deny the review and it will not be eligible to be rendered on the home page

Reset This will reset the state of the review and the review will then need to be re-approved or denied
